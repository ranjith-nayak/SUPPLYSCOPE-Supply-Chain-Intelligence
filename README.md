## Project Overview

SUPPLYSCOPE is an end-to-end Supply Chain Intelligence & Predictive Operations Platform designed to provide complete visibility across the supply chain ecosystem.

Modern organizations often struggle to answer critical questions:

- Why are deliveries delayed?
- Which suppliers are impacting operational performance?
- Which products are likely to face stock shortages?
- Where is inventory overstocked or underutilized?
- How accurate are demand forecasts?
- Which business areas require immediate attention?

SUPPLYSCOPE addresses these challenges through advanced analytics, forecasting models, operational KPIs, and executive-level dashboards.

The platform combines **SQL Analytics, Python Data Processing, Forecasting Techniques, and Interactive Tableau Dashboards** to deliver actionable business insights across inventory, suppliers, logistics, and demand planning.

---

# Business Problem

Supply chain operations generate massive volumes of data across multiple business functions.

Without a centralized intelligence platform, organizations face:

- Inventory imbalances
- Supplier inefficiencies
- Delayed deliveries
- Rising logistics costs
- Poor demand forecasting
- Revenue leakage

The goal of SUPPLYSCOPE is to build a unified analytics solution that transforms operational data into business intelligence for decision-makers.
